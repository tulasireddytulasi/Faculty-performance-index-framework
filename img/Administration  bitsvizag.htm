Administration

Dr. C. V. Gopinath
M.E.,Ph.D.,
Principal

Dr. C. V. Gopinath, was educated in GITAM and Andhra Universities and is an expert in Solid Modeling and Reverse Engineering. He has published research papers in international/national journals/conferences in these areas. He has published books, conducted programs, delivered lectures in various national and international forums. He conducted two AICTE sponsored Staff Development Programs on CAD/CAM.

His vast experience includes 16 years teaching in GITAM University and nine years as Principal of reputed institutes in AP and Odisha. He was instrumental in NBA accreditation for all the departments of JITM (Now Centurion University of Technology and Management), Paralakhemundi, Odisha and Chaitanya Engineering College, Visakhapatnam.

I extend my best wishes to all my students and I am sure that they will create their own path of grand success in life.
